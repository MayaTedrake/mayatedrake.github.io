CAD Final Project

This is my final project for my CAD Modeling class. The assignment was to choose a city in the world and create a pavillion for this city. The city I chose to create a pavillion for Pisa, Italy. An extra addition to the assignment was to use the essence of the sculpture that is in the center of the image. I incorporated the flower and tower aspect of this scuplture and incorporated this into my final project. The pavillion from the top view is in the shape of a flower, around the bottom of the pavillion is a bunch of flowers at different hights to climb on. In the center is a tower that has a ramp to get up. At the top of the tower you have an amazing view, as well as a sight of the flower shapes below.
